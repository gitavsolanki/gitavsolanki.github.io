Task 2 – Creating and Editing

This task checks that you can copy text, edit formatting, insert an image, and save a document correctly.

What you need

  • A word processor (Microsoft Word or Google Docs)
  • The text provided by your tutor
  • The image provided by your tutor

Task instructions

  • a) Download the text provided and image by your tutor
  • b) Copy and paste the text into a word processor
  • c) Change the title so it is:
    • Centred
    • Larger than the rest of the text
  • d) Use at least two different fonts in the document
  • e) Insert the image and align it in the centre below the text
  • f) Change the email address in the text to:
    volunteer@communityproject.org
  • g) Change the date in the document to:
    20 November 2025
  • h) Save the document as:
    YourInitials_Task2
