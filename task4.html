Task 4 – Internet Searching

This task checks that you can search online, compare information, and make a simple decision.

What you need

  • A computer or laptop
  • Internet access
  • A web browser (for example: Chrome or Edge)

Choose an event

You need to travel by train to attend one event.

Event 1 – Wellbeing Event

  • Location: Manchester
  • Date: Next Saturday
  • Time: 12:00 pm – 4:00 pm

Event 2 – Hiking Event

  • Location: Birmingham
  • Date: Next Saturday
  • Time: 11:00 am – 3:00 pm

Steps to complete the task

  1. Decide which event you want to attend.
  2. Open a web browser.
  3. Go to a train website (for example: National Rail).
  4. Search for train journeys from your nearest station to the city of your chosen event.
  5. Look at two different train journeys.
  6. Compare the journey times and departure times.
  7. Decide which journey is best for you.
  8. Take a screenshot showing the two journey options.
  9. Save the screenshot in the folder you created in Task 1.
  10. Save the file as: YourInitials_Task4
