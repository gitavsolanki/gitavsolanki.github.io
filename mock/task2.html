Mock Task 2 – Sending an Email with an Attachment

This task checks that you can send a clear email with an attachment.

What you need

  • An email account (for example: Gmail or Outlook)
  • The screenshot you created in Mock Task 1

Step 1: Open your email

  • Open your email account
  • Click New email or Compose

Step 2: Send the email to your tutor

To:
Enter your tutor’s email address

Subject:
Type exactly:
Mock Task 2 – Email Evidence

Step 3: Write the message

Type the message below into the email:

Hello,

Please find my screenshot from Mock Task 1 attached as evidence.

Kind regards,
Your name

Step 4: Attach your screenshot

  • Click the Attach (paperclip) icon
  • Find your screenshot file
  • Select the file and click Open

Step 5: Check your file name

Before sending, make sure your screenshot is named:

Screenshot Email Evidence

Step 6: Send the email

  • Check the subject line is correct
  • Check the file is attached
  • Click Send
