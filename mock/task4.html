Task 4 – Creating and Formatting a Document

Download the task files:

  • Task4_Files.zip

The file will download to your Downloads folder.

  1. Open your Downloads folder.
  2. Right-click Task4_Files.zip.
  3. Select Extract All.
  4. Open Film_Club_Text.txt.
  5. Copy the text and paste it into a new Word document.
  6. Insert Movie_Image.jpg below the heading.
  7. Change 8pm to 7pm.
  8. Change Saturday to Friday.
  9. Format the document clearly.
  10. Save as Film_Club_Advert in your Mock Test folder.
