About this Assessment Resource

This page has been designed as part of my Assessment Design & Quality Assurance portfolio. It organises the candidate paper, data files and tutor materials into a clear digital assessment portal.

Quality Assurance Focus

  • Assessment readiness: learners can access all required materials in one place.
  • Standardisation: tutors have guidance and mark scheme materials available before delivery.
  • Consistency: the same structure can be reused for future practice assessments.
  • Reflection: learner performance can be reviewed after delivery to improve future resources.
