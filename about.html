About Me & How to Use This Site

About Me

I am a digital skills tutor supporting adult learners to build confidence using computers, the internet, and everyday digital tools.

I teach practical courses that help learners use technology for work, learning, and daily life. My teaching focuses on clear steps, real-life examples, and learning at a comfortable pace.

How to Use This Site

This website is designed to support your learning alongside your classes.

  • Use EDS3 Practice Tasks to practise individual digital skills, such as file management, email, and internet use.
  • Use the Courses page to see the courses you are currently studying.
  • Your tutor will tell you when to use practice tasks or mock tests.
  • You do not need to create an account or log in to use this site.

If you are unsure what to do, ask your tutor for guidance.
