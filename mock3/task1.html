Task 1 – Research a Theme Park

Scenario

You and your friends are planning a day trip to a theme park. You need to research a theme park and collect information to help plan the visit.

Task 1a

Open Microsoft Word.

Create a new document and save it in your DAY TRIP folder.

Use a suitable file name.

Task 1b

Use a search engine to find a theme park you would like to visit.

Take a screenshot showing:

  • Your search words
  • Your search results

Paste the screenshot into your document.

Task 1c

Use the website to find:

  • Theme park name
  • Address
  • Postcode
  • Cost of a day ticket

Add this information to your document.

Save one image showing an attraction or activity from the theme park.

Save the image inside your DAY TRIP folder.

Evidence Required

  • Word document saved in DAY TRIP folder
  • Screenshot of search results
  • Theme park information
  • Theme park image saved in folder

Total for Task 1 = 9 Marks
